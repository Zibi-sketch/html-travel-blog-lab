Adventures in Fire Island

Whether you're a nature enthusiast or have a passion for wild adventures, Fire Island is your place to be.

In my personal experience, there is truly something beautiful in getting the opportunity to view the savannah in it's prime under the gorgeous sunset. Fire Island not only gives guided tours of its vast grasslands, but also has a modern resort perched atop a hill giving visitors a 360 degree view of the entire park with all-round catering services. It is both a relaxing and captivating sight,whether seated with a cup of tea in hand or out driving across the plains in real time.
